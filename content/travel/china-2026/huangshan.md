2 nights, used in [[routes|Routes B, C]]. Huangshan ("Yellow Mountain") is the granite-peak-and-cloud-sea range that defined Chinese landscape painting. The trip is one full day on the mountain (sunrise above the clouds, summit ridges, cable cars) and one slower day in the Huizhou-style villages below — Hongcun and Xidi, both UNESCO sites.

## Getting there

- HSR Hangzhou East → **Huangshan North** (~1h 30, CNY 165 second-class) or Shanghai Hongqiao → **Huangshan North** (~2h 50). Onward to **Tangkou** village (the mountain base) is a 1-hour bus or taxi from the station.
- Onward from Huangshan: HSR Huangshan North → Xiamen North (~5h 30 via the eastern coastal line) or → Shenzhen North (~6h) — long but doable in a single day.

## Mountain logistics — read this first

Huangshan is run as a single ticketed park (CNY 190 entry, ~7 days advance booking via the official WeChat mini-program). You **must** decide where to sleep:

- **On the mountain (recommended for one night)**: stay at one of the 4 summit hotels — **Beihai Hotel** (best central location), **Xihai Hotel** (sunset side), **Paiyunlou** (most renovated), or **Shilin Hotel**. Rooms are basic and overpriced (~$200–350) but you wake up next to the sunrise viewpoint. **Book 2+ months ahead.**
- **In Tangkou at the base**: cheap (~$60), modern, but you lose the sunrise option unless you take the first 06:30 cable car up.
- **In Tunxi Old Town (Huangshan city)** ~70 min from the gate: only worth it if you're combining with a Hongcun/Xidi village day.

Recommended split for 2 nights: **night 1 on the summit (Beihai), night 2 in Tunxi Old Town or Hongcun homestay** for the village morning.

Cable cars (CNY 90 each way, ~10 min) save the brutal 4–6 hr climb. The two main lines:
- **Yungu Cable Car** (east side) — drops you at White Goose Ridge, closest to Beihai Hotel and sunrise spots
- **Yuping Cable Car** (south side) — drops you at the Welcoming Pine, closer to Tangkou

Take Yungu up, Yuping down (or vice-versa) for a circuit.

## Day 1 — Up the mountain, sunset ridge, summit night

- Morning: HSR + transfer Tangkou → Yungu Cable Car base (~30 min by bus). Bus tickets CNY 19. Drop bags at Beihai Hotel (a porter team will bring them up if booked).
- Take **Yungu Cable Car up** to White Goose Ridge.
- Afternoon: walk the **Beihai → Refreshing Terrace → Lion Peak** loop (~3 hr, paved stone steps). This is the central scenic plateau with the classic gnarled-pine-on-granite photographs.
- Sunset at **Refreshing Terrace (Qingliang Tai)** or **Bright Summit (Guangming Ding)** if energy is left.
- Dinner at Beihai Hotel (set menu, sample-sized portions; the food is fine, not the point).
- Lights out early — sunrise wake-up is 04:30–05:00.

## Day 2 — Sunrise + west sea ridge + descend to Hongcun

- **Sunrise** at **Refreshing Terrace** or **Lion Peak**. Bring the down jacket; even in October it's ~0°C up top before dawn.
- After breakfast, walk the **West Sea Grand Canyon** (Xihai Da Xiagu) loop — the most dramatic ridge in the park. There's a small monorail at the bottom (CNY 100) that saves the climb back out; use it.
- Descend via **Yuping Cable Car** to the south base. See the **Welcoming Pine** on the way down (busy, but iconic).
- Bus or pre-booked driver from Tangkou to **Hongcun** (~1.5 hr drive west).
- Check into a Hongcun guesthouse inside the old village walls — **Jing De Tang** or **Shu Yu Lou** are restored Qing-dynasty merchant houses with courtyards.
- Late afternoon: walk Hongcun's **Moon Pond** and **South Lake** before sunset (admission CNY 104, valid 3 days). This is the village reflected in still water; if it's calm, you get the postcard shot.
- Dinner at the guesthouse — most Hongcun guesthouses serve set Huizhou-style meals (stinky mandarin fish, bamboo-shoot stews, salted-pork).

## Day 3 — Xidi morning, depart

- Sunrise walk in Hongcun's alleys before the tour buses arrive (07:00–08:00 is the magic window).
- DiDi or guesthouse-arranged car to **Xidi** (~25 min). Xidi is quieter, denser, and the other UNESCO Huizhou village. Two hours is enough — focus on the ancestral halls and the carved-stone gateway.
- Lunch in Xidi village, then drive back to **Huangshan North Station** (~1 hr 15 from Xidi).
- Onward HSR.

## Booking notes

- **Mountain ticket + cable car**: book 3–7 days ahead on the official WeChat mini-program "黄山旅游" or via Klook (English).
- **Beihai Hotel** (or any summit hotel): book 2+ months ahead, especially for weekends. October is peak.
- **Hongcun guesthouse**: book direct or via Trip.com 4–8 weeks ahead.
- **Weather**: check the 5-day forecast obsessively. A flat-grey rain day on Huangshan is the worst version of this trip. Build a 1-day buffer if you can.

## What to pack for the summit

- Real warm layer (fleece + windbreaker minimum; down for sunrise)
- Hiking shoes — the steps are uneven granite, often wet
- Headlamp for the pre-dawn walk to sunrise
- Small daypack with water + snacks; mountain prices are brutal
- Power bank — phone reception is patchy and you'll burn battery on photos
