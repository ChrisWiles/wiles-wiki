Situational Awareness LP 5020 is useful as a market expression of the [[ai-data-center-buildout-investment-memo|AI data center buildout]] thesis because the disclosed portfolio is not a simple Nvidia proxy. It appears to target bottleneck nodes across power, compute real estate, optics, memory/storage, and semiconductors.

## Fintel Snapshot

Fintel reported the latest 13F as filed on 2026-02-11 for the 2025-12-31 reporting period. The profile listed:

- Portfolio value: about $5.52B.
- Current positions: 29.
- Top holdings: Bloom Energy, CoreWeave calls, Intel calls, Lumentum, CoreWeave common, Core Scientific, IREN, Applied Digital, SanDisk, Cipher, EQT, Coherent, Solaris Energy Infrastructure, and Tower Semiconductor.

Fintel's listed top increases included Bloom Energy, Lumentum, CoreWeave calls, SanDisk, Cipher, Applied Digital, Coherent, Kilroy Realty, EQT, and Tower Semiconductor.

## Portfolio Interpretation

**Power is the scarce asset.** Bloom Energy, Solaris Energy Infrastructure, EQT, Power Solutions International, Liberty Energy, ProPetro, and Babcock & Wilcox point toward speed-to-power and distributed/onsite generation exposure.

**Power-secured compute real estate matters.** CoreWeave, Core Scientific, IREN, Applied Digital, Cipher, Hut 8, Bitdeer, Riot, CleanSpark, and Bitfarms express the idea that power-secured campuses and former crypto infrastructure can be converted into AI capacity.

**Optics are a non-obvious bottleneck.** Lumentum and Coherent suggest the fund sees AI networking and optical interconnect as critical bottlenecks as clusters scale.

**Memory and semiconductors remain central.** SanDisk, Intel calls, and Tower Semiconductor indicate continued exposure to storage, semiconductor capacity, and foundry-related optionality.

**Real estate is selective.** Kilroy Realty exposure may reflect interest in power-adjacent or AI-relevant real estate optionality rather than a generic office REIT thesis. ^[inferred]

## What The Portfolio Does Not Say

The 13F is delayed, incomplete, and excludes undisclosed cash, shorts, private holdings, swaps, and intra-quarter changes. It should not be treated as a current recommended portfolio.

The useful signal is thematic: Situational Awareness LP appears to be investing around scarce infrastructure nodes created by frontier AI scale-up, especially where the public market may not yet classify the company as a first-order AI beneficiary.
