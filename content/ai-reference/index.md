## Core Frame

A post-AGI economy is best treated as a scenario space, not a forecast. The most important question is whether advanced AI remains mostly complementary to human labor or becomes a broad substitute for cognitive, administrative, creative, scientific, and eventually physical work.

If labor stops being the main scarce input, the economic problem shifts from how people earn wages to how people obtain claims on output, capital, compute, housing, land, energy, and other scarce resources.
