This is investment research, not financial advice. Every fair-value range below is a research framework derived from disclosed inputs and stated assumptions — not a price target. Position-sizing guidance assumes a constructive view on the [[ai-data-center-buildout-investment-memo|50-100 GW AI buildout thesis]]; the master tripwires in [[risk-tripwires-and-warning-indicators]] are what would force a re-underwrite.

## How to read this list

- **12-month winners** — catalyst-driven names where backlog conversion, multiple expansion, HBM contract resets, or guidance step-ups can rerate the stock inside 4 quarters. Higher beta, more single-name risk.
- **5-year holds** — durable compounders where the AI buildout is one of multiple structural tailwinds. Lower turnover, anchor positions.
- **At-risk / avoid** — popular AI-buildout names where current valuation, balance-sheet structure, or competitive dynamics make the long thesis precarious. Framed as "avoid / underweight" — not as short calls.
- **Conviction high/medium** — high = primary catalyst is well-disclosed and structurally supplied-constrained; medium = thesis works but has more cyclical or execution dependency.
- **Patel/SA flag** — names that Dylan Patel / SemiAnalysis have publicly called out as binding 2026-2027 bottlenecks. These names get heavier weight in the conviction scoring.
- **Fair-value range** — bear / base / bull from the underlying dossier. "Above base" means current price already exceeds the base case; "below base" means the framework signals upside vs disclosed inputs.

Multiples and prices are as of 2026-04-30. Dossiers should be refreshed after every earnings cycle (the [scheduled quarterly refresh routine](https://claude.ai/code/routines/trig_01GfdY2LB2SmQ7ThZWrGh7yK) handles the scorecard; this picks list will lag by one quarter).

- VRT 12-mo trades on backlog conversion and margin expansion to FY26 guide; VRT 5-yr trades on 5 generations of liquid-cooling adoption and installed-base service annuity.
- PWR 12-mo trades on $48.5B backlog conversion through FY26-FY27; PWR 5-yr trades on multi-decade T&D capex + lineman wage rents.
- GEV 12-mo trades on slot pricing and quarterly orders; GEV 5-yr trades on Power + Wind + Electrification + SMR sum-of-parts re-rating across cycles.
- CEG 12-mo trades on PJM 2027/28 BRA + Calpine integration; CEG 5-yr trades on PPA repricing across 22 reactors + license extensions to 80 years + SMR optionality.

## Position-sizing template

Three sizing frameworks, mix and match by risk tolerance.

**Equal-weight** — each pick gets 1/N of the basket. Simplest; treats all conviction levels the same. Defaults: 11 12-mo picks at ~9% each = 100% of "12-mo sleeve"; 12 5-yr picks at ~8.3% each = 100% of "5-yr sleeve."

**Conviction-weighted** — high-conviction names get 1.5x equal weight, medium get 0.5x. For the 12-mo list:
- 1.5x sleeve: MU, FIX, CEG, MRVL, CRDO (Patel-flagged or clear catalysts)
- 1.0x sleeve: VRT, PWR, COHR
- 0.7x sleeve: GEV, POWL (already above base; trim on rallies)
- 0.5x sleeve: CLF (commodity-cycle pair-hedge candidate)

For the 5-yr list:
- 1.5x sleeve: AVGO, ASML, TSM, BESI, ATEY, SHECY, SLTRY (Patel-flagged semis-equipment + custom ASIC + wafer core)
- 1.0x sleeve: ETN, LIN, CEG, CAT
- 0.7x sleeve: VRT, PWR, GEV (overlap with 12-mo sleeve; size carefully to avoid double-count)
- Wafer sleeve: split SHECY:SLTRY at roughly 60:40 within the wafer-maker allocation; use Frankfurt/Tokyo for size if ADR liquidity is constraining.

**Volatility-adjusted** — divide each name's target weight by realized 60-day volatility. Practical effect:
- Lower-vol anchors (LIN, CEG, ETN, CAT) get bigger weights.
- Higher-vol names (CRDO, POWL, MU, MRVL, CLF) get smaller.
- Result: a more equity-curve-stable basket; loses some upside in the high-beta winners.

**Hedges** — explicit pair trades to consider:
- Long CLF / short auto-OEM basket (hedges HRC commodity exposure).
- Long TSM ADR / Long ASML (geographic concentration hedge for foundry exposure).
- Long VRT / short MOD (relative-value cooling pair — VRT moat-and-scale advantage).
- Long DELL puts or short DELL (small) within an ISG-hedge sleeve.

**Caps and limits**:
- TSM individual position cap at 5% of basket (geopolitical risk overlay).
- Neocloud names (CRWV, APLD, IREN) — combined exposure capped at 0% in this list (all are at-risk / avoid).
- CLF position size ≤ 3% of basket; high-beta commodity exposure means small dollar risk only.
